AIDA - Artificial Intelligence powered Data Analysis

Posted by Eprobito on Jan 12, 2023 OCRNLP. 4 comments

We believe that unlike past technological advances which took a long time to permeate from being the “advantage” of a few large companies to being available to all, the AI (Artificial Intelligence) revolution can and should be made usable by large number of small to medium sized businesses, notwithstanding their size or their technical capabilities. To this end, we have designed a simple, easy to use, no code Business User Interface (BUI as opposed to GUI) that will allow any business user anywhere in the company hierarchy to utilize the latest in AI tools to define and solve business problems at the operational, tactical and strategic levels.

Check it out at ezaitool.com

People empowered with access to and understanding of data can propel an organization to phenomenal success. Our goal is to provide the tool(s) needed by the business user to be able to understand the data and make sound business decisions.

Data is either structured (like data from a database or any ordered, formatted source with labels) or unstructured (documents - text or pdf, images, videos). The first step is to obtain the data / documents and the next step is to process the data to generate output that can be used in the business process.

In today’s world of Artificial Intelligence (AI), data takes on a much larger role and data is needed for training models that can then predict with an astonishing level of accuracy what may happen and suggest what steps need to be taken. And here is where data cleansing, synthetic data and data labeling come to fore. Decisions made by a domain expert supersede those of a statistician / data scientist mainly because the context is crucial all the time. Our BUI allows the business user to decide how the data is cleanses and where synthetic data is to be used and the tool allows for automated data labeling in certain cases.
