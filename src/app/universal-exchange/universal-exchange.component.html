Universal Exchange - Bringing Liquidity to Illiquid Assets

Posted by Eprobito on Jan 12, 2023 OCRNLP. 4 comments

The Universal Exchange is a platform for peer-to-peer purchase and sale of alternative assets. The key aspects of this Exchange are:

  • Peer to Peer
  • SIngle Platform across multiple alternative assets
  • Unitization of alternative asset investments
  • Auction and Reverse Auction Mechanisms
  • Smart Contracts
  • Valuation and Provenance Services
