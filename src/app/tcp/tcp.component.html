The Capstone Platform: TCP - An integrated Insurance Policy Servicing Platform

Posted by Eprobito on Jan 12, 2023 TCP. 4 comments

The platform supports

A. Policy Management Services

  • Request and analyze premium illustrations to support model parameters including utilizing no lapse guarantee benefits.
  • Review annual statements and optimize premium projection based on agreed parameters annually.
  • Deliver premium schedule
  • Confirm carrier receipt of premium

B. Insured Tracking Services

  • Semi annual contact with insured/representative/physician
  • Sweep data bases weekly
  • Coordinate additional services as needed for tracking insured

C. Death Claim Processing

  • assist in obtaining death certificates
  • assist in obtaining death cert packet from carrier
  • guide client through execution of the packet
  • Help manage claim process through carrier distribution of the death benefit

D. Life expectancy review

  • Obtain LE reports (where available and permitted)
